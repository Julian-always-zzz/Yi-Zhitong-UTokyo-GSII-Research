# Research Portfolio

## 1. Graduation Project

### Design and Implementation of a Public Opinion Monitoring System for Scenic Areas

This graduation project focuses on intelligent public opinion monitoring and decision support for scenic areas under the context of smart tourism.

The research introduces Situation Awareness Theory and integrates multiple artificial intelligence and information system technologies to construct an end-to-end public opinion monitoring framework, including:

- Multi-source social media data collection
- Natural language processing
- BERT-based sentiment analysis
- BERTopic-based topic modeling
- Risk assessment
- Decision support
- Django-based information system implementation

## 2. Research Paper

### Carbon Emission Effects of Electric Vehicle Charging Behavior Based on Dynamic Carbon Emission Factors

This research investigates the carbon emission effects of electric vehicle charging behavior by incorporating dynamic carbon emission factors into the evaluation framework.

The study provides quantitative analysis for low-carbon transportation and sustainable energy management.

## 3. Undergraduate Research Project

This undergraduate research project proposes a community emergency mutual assistance application that utilizes resident information to improve emergency response efficiency and community resilience.
